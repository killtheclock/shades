### Basics

## Early Strategy
1. Familiarise yourself with everything.
2. Play at least 3–4 times per day.
3. Participate in all events.
4. Red dots indicate something new.
5. Build 5 strong heroes and upgrade HQ fast.
6. Prioritise Full Preparedness daily.
7. Focus on VS.
8. Check Alliance, Notice, and Personal chat.
9. Donate Zents to Alliance Tech daily.
10. Do all Radar events except radioactive ones.
11. Do Bounty Missions daily.
12. Do Hero Battlefield up to level 19.
13. Do Furylord daily.
14. Do Danger Lurks daily.
15. Send teams to gather resources.
16. Learn team and city wall mechanics.
17. Check shops daily.
18. Use Zents wisely.
19. Buy growth items only.
20. Merit Shop: orange & purple.
21. Glory Shop: unique items.
22. Spend diamonds only in Lucky Discounter.
23. Target purple & orange trucks.
24. Always be building.
25. Always be researching.
26. Collect free items.
27. Click Help Alliance.
28. Donate to Alliance.
29. Save gifts until 10.
